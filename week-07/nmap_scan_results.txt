2. Why is it dangerous to run an intense scan (like -A) on a fragile legacy system?
Answer:The -A flag is "aggressive" because it launches multiple complex tasks at once: an  old server's processing power can be quickly overwhelmed by these simultaneous requests, causing the system to freeze, crash, or stop responding to legitimate users.
